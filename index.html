Origins

My origins begin is christchurch in a quant black house down a coldesac near the university. Both my parents love to tinker, dad, a lecturer at the University loves wood working and gardening while my mother a radiologist spent a great deal of time in the sewing room. Their creativity, curiosity and strong opinions created two very chaotic and rebellious children, made worse by their sprinkling of Dyslexia and ADHD. My bother now works as a Quantity Surveyor and is obsessed with F1, History & Classics, and Skiing. Unlikely most people I wont delve into my Highschool, I was pretty reserved and most thankfully for the great fellas from the school who are still with me. University I launched a homebrewing society, becoming UCSA’s public enemy 1#, before transitioning to trying startups and taking the lead role for the student led startup society.

Passions

My hobbies and passions often change, and I’m always trying new things. When I’ve got the money, I love getting up the slopes going to live concerts or checking in with close friends. One passion, I never thought would stick is Salsa which I’ve ended up really enjoying due to the social nature, and it being an alien experience to my day to day. For fitness I kickbox because it keeps my mind engaged and a regular gym is too monotinous.
