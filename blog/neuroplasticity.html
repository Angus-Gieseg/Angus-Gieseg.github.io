What is this blog post?

The purpose of the this blog is to discuss and present my takeaways from researching Neuroplasticity and Growth Mindset.

Neuroplasticity - Sentis

The main take-aways from this video was that our brain is not static, the old-age concept that when your younger is the only time you can learn, and that your abilities and skills are tied to your intellect.

How neuroplasticity helps you shape your brain - Lara Boyd

The best drive to increase your neuroplasticity is done through behavior - strong habits, mindset towards focusing and embracing challenges. Its highly variable. Consistent practice and doing the work will make you learn faster, if you struggle or feel uncomfortable there is greater learning. Its shaped both by what you choose to do and what you don't choose. There are certain genetic characteristics that help learning in different styles for different content.
