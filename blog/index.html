The purpose of this manifestor is to make a public Ambiguous declaration of commitment to move overseas and experience a strong product culture. Over the next 15 weeks I hope to refine my vision and commit to working outside of the safe area code I've lived my entire life.

Family

I was born and raised in Christchurch from an academic father and mother who works in healthcare who both have committed to long stints living overseas. Having a academic father meant I my curiousity was sustained and was encouraged to freely think and never shy away from challenges. My mother was distinctly different to my father, she was extroverted and was genuinely curious about people which inspired me. I have a diverse group of friend ranging different backgrounds and life stages who I often finding myself leaning on for guidance.
